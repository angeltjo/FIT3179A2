Global Population

This map shows the Global Gender Equality Score for countries
all over the world. The higher the score is, the
more gender equal the country is. From the map, we can see that the most gender equal country is Iceland meaning the number of females to males is more even than other countries. The least gender equal country is Yemen.

This graph shows the global distribution of females and males by ages. In the younger ages, there are more males than females however in the older age ranges, there are more females than males. All together, there is a greater global population of males than females.
